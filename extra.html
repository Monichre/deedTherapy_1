Welcome, I'm Mark Wildermann

I have a significantly wide array of work experience: as a private tutor of students at the elementary through high school grades and as a Professional Writing Tutor at college; as a teacher in a high school special education program; as a college Campus Safety Officer - emergency first responder, student/staff/faculty/family liaison, and men's group facilitator; and, as a hypnotherapist supporting an adult population with issues such as smoking, body image, relationships, sport and career performance, and physical and emotional trauma.

At your first visit we will initiate and develop a trusting relationship while we consider what you immediately present as your concerns. This time-honored experience of conversation allows the client and the therapist to unfold, understand, and resolve in the most natural process of talk therapy all the ordinary problems of living. Then we choose the most ideal practices, techniques and exercises to address specific needs through developing and strengthening one's own resources. I recognize the individual client as the only one who really knows him or herself; it is my responsibility to help them make best use of that self-knowledge.
